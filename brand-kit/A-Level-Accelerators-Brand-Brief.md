## About the Brand

**A-Level Accelerators** helps UK sixth-form students achieve top A-Level grades through a structured study system developed by Dr Waleed Ahmad. The brand positions itself as the premium, expert-led route to top results — aspirational but warm, authoritative but approachable.

**Tagline direction:** "Top 1% Study System" — results-focused, confidence-building.

**Target audience:** Year 12 and Year 13 A-Level students (ages 16–18) in the UK, and their parents.

**Tone of voice:**
- Confident and authoritative (backed by Dr Waleed's expertise)
- Warm and encouraging — never intimidating
- Premium but accessible — never stuffy or elitist
- Clear and direct — students are busy; get to the point
- British English spelling throughout (colour, recognise, programme, etc.)

## Typography

### Headings (h1, h2, h3, display, prices)
- **Font:** Georgia (system serif)
- **Google Fonts equivalent:** Lora or Playfair Display
- **Weight:** Bold (700) or Regular (400) depending on context
- **Colour:** Brand Purple `#2E2557` on light backgrounds; White or Cream on dark backgrounds
- **Large price figures use Georgia at 50px+ in Brand Gold**

### Body copy, nav, buttons, labels, UI
- **Font:** System sans-serif — Arial, Helvetica, -apple-system, Roboto
- **Google Fonts equivalent:** Inter
- **Weight:** Regular (400) for body; Semibold (600) for nav links; Bold (700–800) for CTAs and pills
- **Colour:** Text `#1A1535` for body; Purple `#2E2557` for nav links

### Specific text treatments
- Section eyebrow labels: ALL CAPS, wide letter-spacing (0.2em+), Gold `#C9A96E`, small sans-serif
- Pricing tier names: Georgia, 27–29px, bold, Purple
- CTA buttons: Arial/sans, 14–16px, weight 800, letter-spacing 0.02em, Gold background + Purple text
- Earlybird / badge pills: rounded-full, Gold background, Purple text, weight 800, uppercase

## UI Style

- **Border radius:** Cards use 12px; buttons use 8px; pills/badges use 9999px (full)
- **Card style:** White or Cream background, 2px Gold border, 12px radius, subtle purple shadow
- **Featured/highlighted cards:** 3px Gold border, Gold glow box-shadow
- **Section backgrounds alternate:** Cream → White → light gradient (cream to white)
- **Dividers:** 1px, Gold at 30% opacity (`rgba(201,169,110,0.3)`)
- **Tick/check marks:** Gold ✓ character, weight 900

## What to avoid

- Don't use generic stock-photo aesthetics — keep it sharp and premium
- Don't make it feel like a tutoring agency — this is a *system*, not 1:1 tutoring
- Don't use cool blues or greens — the palette is strictly purple, gold, cream
- Don't use lightweight or thin fonts in CTAs — buttons should feel bold and confident
- Don't write in American English — British spelling throughout
