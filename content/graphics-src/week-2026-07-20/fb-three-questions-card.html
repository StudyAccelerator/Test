A-LEVEL ACCELERATORS

Three questions that work better than "How's revision going?"

1

"Which topic are you avoiding at the moment?"

Every student has one. It's usually where the grade is hiding.

2

"When you revise something, how do you know it's gone in?"

"I read it and it makes sense" is the warning sign. Reading feels like learning. Exams test remembering.

3

"If the exam were tomorrow, which paper would worry you most?"

More useful than any school report you'll read this year.

None of them mention hours. Effort is almost never the problem.
