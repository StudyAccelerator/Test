A-LEVEL ACCELERATORS

University offers are based on predicted grades, not final results.

Teachers write predictions early in the autumn term of Year 13.
They lean heavily on how Year 12 ended and how Year 13 starts.
So for a student going into Year 13, this summer quietly sets the UCAS options list.

The useful response isn't a summer chained to a desk. It's about two focused hours a day, on the right topics.
