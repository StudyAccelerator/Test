# Partnerships

The repeatable process lives in `.claude/skills/partnership-outreach/`. Point it at a company and it produces a research dossier plus a draft outreach email in a folder here. Waleed approves and sends everything himself: nothing in this folder has been sent unless the index below says so.

Each target gets `content/partnerships/<company-slug>/` containing:

- `research.md`: the dossier. Business model, honestly sourced scale, the pressure-tested gap, the designed trade, the named decision maker with routes, and the questions only Waleed can answer.
- `outreach-email.md`: the draft email, subject options, LinkedIn note, follow-up nudge, pre-send checklist and send log.
