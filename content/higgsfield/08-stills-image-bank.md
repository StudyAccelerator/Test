# 08. Stills image bank (Soul)

Photo prompts for Soul 2.0. Rules: no text baked into any image (models mangle it; overlays go on in Canva), brand palette woven through props and light rather than filters, and every people shot carries natural-photo language so nothing reads as AI. Where a picture needs headline space, the prompt reserves it.

Reminder from the visual guide: on LinkedIn and organic Facebook, REAL photos of Waleed beat everything. These stills are for Meta ads, blog headers, email banners and backplates, where illustrative photography is normal.
