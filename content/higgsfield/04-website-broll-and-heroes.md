# 04. Website b-roll and hero loops

Ambient video for the site. Rules for every prompt here: 16:9 horizontal unless marked, motion kept slow and subtle so headline text can sit on top, colours locked to the brand (cream #FBF8F3 and #F3EBD8 fields, deep purple #2E2557 and gold #C9A96E accents), and each clip should loop cleanly without a visible cut. Ask for 8 to 10 seconds and trim the loop point in post. Nothing here goes live by itself; these are assets for Waleed to place.

---
