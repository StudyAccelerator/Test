# 03. Results day pack

A-level results day is **Thursday 13 August 2026, results at 8am** (GCSE follows Thursday 20 August). This is the biggest brand moment of the year. Half of this creative supports current Year 13s and their parents on the day; half pivots Year 12 families to "your year starts now", which feeds the Summer Accelerator and the diagnostic.

Tone rules: empathetic, steady, genuinely useful. Never gloating, never doom. No celebrations framed as "our students' results" because there is no results data until August 2026. No fake UCAS screens, no readable grade sheets.
